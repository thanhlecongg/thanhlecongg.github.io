Currently

First-year Ph.D. Student at CIS, The University of Melbourne as a Ph.D Student under the advisor of Dr. Bach Le and Prof. Toby Murray. I'm interested in improving developer productivity by designing automatic ways of software debugging. In particular, I want to create novel techniques to support developer in detecting, managing and fixing of bug/vulnerabilities. Currently, I am focusing on trustworthiness of automated program repair.
