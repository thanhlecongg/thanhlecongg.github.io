Hi! I’m Thanh Le-Cong (Lê Công Thành in Vietnamese), a Ph.D. Student at [CIS, The University of Melbourne](https://www.cis.unimelb.edu.au/), advised by [Dr. Bach Le](https://xuanbachle.github.io/) and [Prof. Toby Murray](https://people.eng.unimelb.edu.au/tobym/). Before joining UoM, I was a research engineer at [SOAR (SOftware Analytic Research)](https://soarsmu.github.io/), [Singapore Management University](https://smu.edu.sg/) under the advisor of [Prof. David Lo](http://www.mysmu.edu/faculty/davidlo/). I'm interested in improving developer productivity by designing automatic ways of software debugging. In particular, I want to create novel techniques to support developer in detection, localization and fixing of bug/vulnerabilities. Currently, I am focusing on robust and trustworthy automated program repair. 

## Research interests

+ Automated Program Repair,
+ Mining Software Repositories,
+ Artifical Intellgence for Software Engineering,
+ Neural Software Analysis
